Our Services

Comprehensive customs clearance for both imports and exports, covering all necessary documentation, compliance with customs formalities, and liaison with various authorities. The service extends to container consolidations, LCL/FCL freight booking, and other essential customs-related tasks like duty calculations and bank guarantee execution.

Specialized freight forwarding and shipping services including providing competitive freight rates, ensuring efficient ocean and air transportation across multiple locations, and offering time-definite delivery services, all tailored to meet the specific needs of their clients.

Solutions tailored to the requirements of clients for their import and export cargo. Emphasis is placed on the safe and secure delivery of materials, emphasizing the trust and expectations of customers.

Comprehensive warehousing solutions including general storage, inventory management, and container stuffing/de-stuffing. This service aims to improve clients' inventory management and reduce operating costs while supporting both inbound and outbound logistics.

Project cargo handling, special ODC clearances, pre-shipment advice, documentation, and various audits and certifications. We also provide consultancy, insurance, packing, palletization, and fumigation services.

Clearance services for a diverse range of products including pharmaceuticals, chemicals, bulk drugs, food and nutraceuticals, textiles, engineering equipment, automobile equipment, and sanitary ware, ensuring compliance and efficiency in handling diverse cargo types.
